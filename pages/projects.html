Integrated Pharmacy Projects

We offer comprehensive solutions for pharmacy owners from design to operation

Our Integrated Pharmacy Services:

  • Obtaining necessary licenses and completing all governmental procedures
  • Interior and exterior finishing with the latest modern designs
  • Installation of advertising signs using high-quality materials (MDF, HDF, cladding)
  • Licensing of advertising signs from relevant municipalities
  • Preparation of purchase and sale contracts and property transfer procedures
  • Registration of contracts in the real estate registry
  • Establishment of pharmacy chains and issuance of commercial registration
  • Issuance of tax card and completion of financial procedures
  • Stocking the pharmacy with medicines and medical supplies
  • Coding medicines on specialized computer systems
  • Providing specialized management and labor
  • Handing over the pharmacy fully ready for operation

Successful Projects:

We have delivered and completed pharmacies for Dr. Ayman Ali Othman, Secretary-General of the Pharmacists Syndicate of Egypt, Oxygen agency pharmacies in Egypt, and other successful projects that achieved our clients' full satisfaction.
